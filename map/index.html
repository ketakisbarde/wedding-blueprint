Venue Notes

  • Chateau has two floors — Ground and First. Ground floor has some rooms, Mehendi main hall, and tables for games & activities for guests. The outside welcome sign (colorful disco balls, golden stand) is by the walkway entrance.
  • First floor has bridal and groom suites, an inside welcome sign (taken care of by Sabrina), plus a table with printed invites, props & mementos from close personal friends — decorated.

  • 4:00 PM onwards — DJ / Sangeet. Shove away seating to make space for dance.
